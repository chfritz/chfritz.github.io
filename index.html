I describe myself as a full-stack artificial intelligence (AI) research developer and tech lead. I am experienced leading teams of both researchers and software engineers but am hand-on and have development experience along the full stack. I chose this as my thesis topic after my previous, frustrating experience trying to make real robots score goals in robotic soccer.
